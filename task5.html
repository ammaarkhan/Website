Home


This image of me was taken over 4 years ago but it feels like last month. It's from a family outing to Half-Moon Beach in Dammam, Saudi Arabia. We had gone quad biking in the sand dunes just before hitting the beach. At the time, I was enjoying the last few days of my summer break before starting grade 10. 4 years later, this picture brings back warm memories with my parents and siblings at home. Best part about this image, believe it or not, is that I wasn't posing for a picture here, my dad just snapped it as I mindlessly kicked the football.
